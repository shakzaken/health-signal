Lifestyle & Diet Notes — Maya Cohen — 2024
==========================================

A record of intentional lifestyle changes made during 2024 alongside the supplement and health journey.

---

April 8, 2024

Started a 30-minute morning walk routine. The idea was simple: get outside before work, every weekday, before breakfast. I was skeptical I'd stick to it — I'm not a morning person — but it's been two weeks and I've managed 8 out of 10 days. The fresh air does genuinely help me feel more awake.

I read that light exposure in the morning helps regulate cortisol and sleep cycles. Whether or not that's the reason, I notice I'm getting tired earlier in the evening and falling asleep faster. That alone feels worth it.

Current symptoms at this point: still tired, joint stiffness improving slightly, headaches 2-3x per week.

---

May 20, 2024

Made a deliberate shift in diet this month. Cutting back on processed food — mainly biscuits, crackers, and ready-made sauces that I was eating mindlessly. Replacing with more whole foods: lentils, spinach, eggs, sardines (read they're good for omega-3 and vitamin D), sweet potato, and more leafy greens in general.

Not a dramatic overhaul — just moving the dial. I'm still eating normally at restaurants and social occasions. But during the week, the pantry looks different.

Not sure yet if it's making a difference to how I feel. The joint stiffness has been improving over the past month and a half. Hard to say what's driving it — the iron, the Vitamin D, the diet, the morning walks, or the omega-3. Probably a combination.

---

July 4, 2024

Joined a Thursday evening yoga class at a local studio near work. The class is described as "gentle flow" — suitable for beginners and people with joint issues. I was a bit self-conscious at first but now I look forward to it.

The instructor emphasizes slow movement, breath work, and gentle joint mobilization. My knees and fingers feel noticeably better after each session. I've also started doing 10 minutes of stretching on my own on Tuesday mornings, mostly what I've learned in class.

Energy has been much better since June. The morning stiffness that was a daily struggle in January is now occasional and short-lived.

---

October 5, 2024

Reflecting on the year. What I've changed:
- Morning walks: 30 minutes, 4-5 days per week (slips to 3 in busy weeks)
- Diet: more whole foods, fewer processed snacks, more leafy greens and legumes
- Yoga: weekly class + some home practice
- Earlier bedtime: aiming for 10:30pm vs 11:30-midnight in January
- Supplements: Vitamin D, iron (now stopped), omega-3, magnesium

What's improved:
- Energy: dramatically better
- Morning stiffness: from daily 20-minute stiffness to occasional 5 minutes
- Brain fog: mostly resolved
- Mood: much more stable and positive
- Headaches: reduced from 2-3/week to occasionally

What I'm still working on:
- Vitamin D still a bit borderline even on 4000 IU — will discuss with doctor
- Want to build strength more systematically, considering adding resistance training in 2025
- Still find I dip in energy during very stressful work periods

The combination of medical treatment (supplements for deficiencies) and lifestyle changes has made a significant difference. It's hard to isolate what worked — probably all of it together.
