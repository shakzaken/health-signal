Personal Health Diary — Maya Cohen
January – April 2024

---

January 8, 2024

I've been feeling exhausted for weeks now. Not just regular tiredness — the kind where you sleep 8 hours and wake up feeling like you haven't slept at all. Getting through a full workday feels like a real effort. I used to go for walks in the evening but lately I can't be bothered. My husband keeps asking if I'm okay and honestly I'm not sure.

Also noticing some stiffness in my fingers and knees in the mornings. It takes maybe 20 minutes before I feel like I can move normally. Not painful exactly, more like everything is sluggish.

---

January 19, 2024

Morning stiffness again today. Fingers especially. By 10am it usually passes but it's becoming a daily thing. Had a headache most of yesterday afternoon that I couldn't shake. Took paracetamol and it helped a bit.

Brain feels foggy lately — struggling to concentrate at work, losing my train of thought mid-sentence. My colleagues probably think I'm distracted. I hope it's just stress from the new project.

---

February 5, 2024

Went to see Dr. Levi today. He ordered blood tests — said he wants to check iron, vitamin D, thyroid, and inflammation markers. Not too worried but good to have a baseline. The stiffness and tiredness have been going on long enough that I wanted to get it looked at.

---

February 12, 2024

Got the blood test results back. Vitamin D is low (18, should be 30+), ferritin is low (11, should be above 15), and something called CRP is elevated (12.4, should be under 5). Hemoglobin is also slightly low. The doctor said this might explain why I'm so tired — low iron stores and possible low-grade inflammation.

He's suggesting Vitamin D supplement, iron supplement, and a follow-up in 4 months. Feeling a bit relieved to have an explanation, even if it's not perfect news.

---

February 25, 2024

Started Vitamin D3 (2000 IU per day) and iron bisglycinate (25mg per day) as Dr. Levi recommended. Also started omega-3 fish oil after reading it might help with inflammation. Hoping to notice a difference in a few weeks.

Still very tired. Mood has been low this month — hard to feel motivated about anything. Going to try to get to bed earlier and see if that helps.

---

March 14, 2024

Three weeks on the supplements. Honestly can't tell much difference yet. The morning stiffness is still there — some days better, some days worse. Knees have been bothering me a bit more this week, maybe because I walked more on the weekend. Fatigue continues. My sleep is okay, I think 7-8 hours most nights, but I wake up unrefreshed.

Headaches are happening maybe 2-3 times a week. Not severe but annoying.

---

April 2, 2024

Starting to feel a slight improvement? Hard to say if it's real or wishful thinking. Morning stiffness seems to be lasting about 15 minutes instead of 20. Energy is still not great but I had one day last week where I went for a short walk in the evening without forcing myself.

Decided to also try reducing processed food. Eating more leafy greens, eggs, and legumes. Probably can't hurt.

---

April 22, 2024

Definitely something shifting. Joint stiffness is noticeably shorter in the mornings — more like 10 minutes now. I've been going on short walks 3 times a week. Energy still comes and goes but the worst days feel less frequent. Still getting occasional headaches but brain fog seems slightly less constant.

Looking forward to the follow-up blood test in June to see if the numbers have changed.
