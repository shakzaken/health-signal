Supplement Log — Maya Cohen — 2024
=====================================

Started after blood test results of 08/02/2024 showed low Vitamin D, low ferritin, and elevated CRP.

---

VITAMIN D3 (cholecalciferol)
Brand: Solgar
Dose: 2000 IU per day
Form: softgel capsule
Started: February 25, 2024
Reason: Blood test showed Vitamin D at 18 ng/mL (ref 30-100). Doctor recommended supplementation.
Note: Dose increased to 4000 IU per day on June 28, 2024 after follow-up test showed only minor improvement to 22 ng/mL.
Current status: Ongoing as of November 2024 (4000 IU/day)

---

IRON BISGLYCINATE
Brand: Solgar Gentle Iron
Dose: 25 mg per day
Form: capsule (bisglycinate form — gentler on stomach than ferrous sulfate)
Started: March 3, 2024
Reason: Ferritin at 11 µg/L (ref 15-150), Hemoglobin slightly low at 11.8 g/dL. Doctor recommended iron supplementation.
Timing: Taken in the morning on empty stomach with vitamin C (orange juice) to improve absorption.
Note: No significant GI side effects with bisglycinate form.
Stopped: October 20, 2024
Reason for stopping: November 2024 blood test (taken November 14) confirmed ferritin normalized to 28 µg/L and hemoglobin 13.1 g/dL. Discussed with Dr. Levi — agreed to stop and recheck in 6 months.

---

OMEGA-3 FISH OIL
Brand: Nordic Naturals Ultimate Omega
Dose: 1000 mg per day (EPA 650mg / DHA 450mg)
Form: softgel capsule
Started: March 3, 2024
Reason: Self-initiated. Read about omega-3 and inflammation — wanted to try alongside the iron and Vitamin D given elevated CRP (12.4 mg/L).
Note: No side effects. CRP normalized by November 2024 (2.3 mg/L) — unclear how much fish oil contributed vs. other changes.
Current status: Ongoing as of November 2024

---

MAGNESIUM GLYCINATE
Brand: Now Foods
Dose: 200 mg per day
Form: capsule
Started: July 15, 2024
Reason: Self-initiated after reading about magnesium and sleep quality / muscle tension. Seemed relevant given joint stiffness and sleep issues.
Timing: Taken before bed.
Note: Sleep quality seems improved — hard to attribute to magnesium specifically given other changes at the same time.
Current status: Ongoing as of November 2024
