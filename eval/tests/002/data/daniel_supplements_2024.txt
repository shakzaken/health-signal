Supplement Log — Daniel Bergman — 2024
=======================================

Started after blood test results of 05/03/2024 showed borderline hypothyroid, high cholesterol,
borderline pre-diabetic glucose, and low Vitamin D.

---

VITAMIN D3 (cholecalciferol)
Brand: NOW Foods
Dose: 3000 IU per day (initial)
Form: softgel capsule
Started: March 20, 2024
Reason: Blood test showed Vitamin D at 19 ng/mL (ref 30–100). Dr. Rosen recommended supplementation.
Note: Dose increased to 4000 IU per day on August 12, 2024. August retest showed only improvement to
28 ng/mL on 3000 IU — still below normal range. Dr. Rosen advised increasing the dose.
December 2024 retest: 33 ng/mL — finally in normal range on 4000 IU.
Current status: Ongoing as of December 2024 (4000 IU/day)

---

OMEGA-3 FISH OIL
Brand: Nordic Naturals Ultimate Omega
Dose: 2000 mg per day (EPA 1300mg / DHA 900mg — split across 2 capsules)
Form: softgel capsule
Started: March 20, 2024
Reason: Triglycerides at 168 mg/dL (ref <150) and elevated CRP. Dr. Rosen recommended Omega-3
for triglyceride reduction and anti-inflammatory effect.
Note: Triglycerides improved from 168 (March) to 142 (August) to 128 (December).
CRP also normalized. Contribution of Omega-3 vs. diet changes is unclear.
Current status: Ongoing as of December 2024

---

SELENIUM
Brand: Thorne Research
Dose: 100 mcg per day
Form: capsule
Started: April 1, 2024
Reason: Self-initiated. Read about selenium's role in thyroid hormone conversion (T4 → T3).
Wanted to support thyroid function alongside dietary changes given TSH was elevated.
Not prescribed by Dr. Rosen. She was aware and had no objection.
Note: TSH improved from 4.4 (March) to 3.1 (August) to 2.4 (December). Attribution unclear —
dietary improvement, weight loss, Vitamin D, and selenium may all have contributed.
Current status: Ongoing as of December 2024

---

MAGNESIUM GLYCINATE
Brand: Now Foods
Dose: 300 mg per day
Form: capsule
Started: April 15, 2024
Reason: Self-initiated. Read about magnesium and sleep quality, muscle function, and constipation.
All three were concerns at the time.
Timing: Taken 30 minutes before bed.
Note: Constipation resolved by June–July 2024. Sleep quality improved. Hard to attribute to
magnesium specifically given multiple simultaneous changes.
Current status: Ongoing as of December 2024

---

COQ10 (Ubiquinone)
Brand: Jarrow Formulas
Dose: 200 mg per day
Form: softgel capsule
Started: May 5, 2024
Reason: Self-initiated. Read about CoQ10 and cardiovascular health, mitochondrial energy
production. Relevant given high cholesterol and persistent fatigue at time of starting.
Note: No obvious side effects. Energy improvement observed over May–July but timeline overlaps
with many other changes (diet, exercise, Vitamin D, magnesium). Cannot isolate CoQ10's contribution.
Current status: Ongoing as of December 2024
