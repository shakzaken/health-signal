# HealthSignal

## Product Overview

**HealthSignal** is a personal health intelligence product that helps users understand their health data, track changes over time, and turn scattered medical information into clear, useful insights.

The product is designed for people who upload blood tests, lab results, symptom notes, supplement history, diet information, and other health-related documents, and want help making sense of everything in one place.

HealthSignal is not a replacement for a doctor. Its purpose is to help users become more organized, better informed, and better prepared for medical appointments.

---

## Product Vision

Most people have health information spread across PDFs, lab portals, doctor notes, screenshots, personal notes, supplement lists, and memory.

HealthSignal brings this information together and helps the user answer questions like:

- What changed in my blood tests over time?
- Which markers are abnormal or borderline?
- What patterns appear between my symptoms, diet, supplements, and lab results?
- What should I ask my doctor?
- What health areas should I pay closer attention to?

The goal is to transform confusing health data into a clear personal health timeline.

---

## Target Users

HealthSignal is built for users who are actively trying to understand and improve their health.

Typical users may include:

- People who do frequent blood tests
- People with recurring symptoms
- People who take supplements and want to track effects
- People experimenting with diet or lifestyle changes
- People recovering from environmental exposure, deficiencies, fatigue, or chronic issues
- People who want to prepare better for doctor appointments

---

## Core User Problem

Health data is often hard to understand because it is:

- Fragmented across many files and sources
- Filled with medical terminology
- Difficult to compare across time
- Missing context about symptoms, diet, and lifestyle
- Hard to turn into practical questions for a healthcare provider

Users often remember that “something was low” or “a test changed,” but they do not have a clear picture of the full trend.

HealthSignal solves this by organizing health information around the user, not around isolated documents.

---

## Core Product Experience

### 1. Upload Health Data

The user can upload health-related information such as:

- Blood test results
- Lab reports
- Doctor summaries
- Supplement lists
- Symptom notes
- Diet notes
- Personal health journals

HealthSignal helps turn these uploads into a structured personal health record.

---

### 2. Build a Personal Health Timeline

HealthSignal organizes the user’s health data over time.

The user can see:

- When each test was taken
- Which markers changed
- Which symptoms appeared during different periods
- Which supplements, diet changes, or lifestyle changes happened around the same time

This helps the user move from isolated data points to a timeline-based view of their health.

---

### 3. Understand Lab Results

HealthSignal explains lab results in simple language.

The product can help the user understand:

- Which values are low, high, or borderline
- What each marker generally represents
- Whether a value improved or worsened compared to previous tests
- Which markers may be worth discussing with a doctor

The explanations should be careful, educational, and non-diagnostic.

---

### 4. Detect Health Patterns

HealthSignal helps identify possible patterns across different types of user data.

Examples:

- Symptoms appearing after certain supplements
- Fatigue improving or worsening after diet changes
- Lab markers changing after lifestyle changes
- Repeated deficiencies across multiple tests
- Clusters of symptoms that may deserve medical attention

The product should present these as observations, not medical conclusions.

---

### 5. Prepare for Doctor Appointments

One of the most important product goals is helping users communicate better with healthcare professionals.

HealthSignal can generate:

- A summary of recent health changes
- A list of abnormal or changing lab markers
- A timeline of symptoms
- Questions to ask the doctor
- A concise report the user can bring to an appointment

This makes appointments more focused and productive.

---

## Example User Questions

HealthSignal should help users ask natural questions such as:

- “What changed in my latest blood test?”
- “Which of my markers are still low?”
- “Did my B12 improve over time?”
- “What symptoms did I report around the time my vitamin D was low?”
- “What should I ask my doctor about these results?”
- “Can you summarize my health situation from the last six months?”
- “Which supplements seemed to cause side effects?”
- “What should I monitor in my next blood test?”

---

## Product Principles

### User-Centered

HealthSignal should focus on the user’s personal health story, not just individual lab values.

### Clear and Calm

Health explanations should be easy to understand and should avoid creating panic.

### Evidence-Aware

The product should distinguish between strong evidence, weak evidence, and personal observations.



### Privacy-First

Health data is sensitive. The product should treat user information as private, personal, and protected.

### Timeline-Oriented

The product should emphasize changes over time, because trends are often more useful than single results.

> “What is going on with my health over time, and what should I pay attention to?”
