Personal Health Diary — Maya Cohen
July – October 2024

---

July 3, 2024

Got the June blood test results a few days ago and they're genuinely encouraging. Hemoglobin is back to normal. CRP dropped from 12.4 all the way to 4.1 — almost in range. Ferritin improved from 11 to 18 (still on the low side but heading in the right direction). The one disappointment is Vitamin D — only went up to 22 from 18. Dr. Levi is increasing my dose to 4000 IU per day.

Energy-wise I've been feeling noticeably better compared to January. Still not 100% but there are real good days now. Morning stiffness is down to maybe 5-10 minutes on most days, sometimes none at all.

---

July 18, 2024

Joined a yoga class on Thursday evenings. It was a bit of a push to commit to something but I'm glad I did. The instructor focuses a lot on gentle joint mobility which feels good for my knees and fingers. Also just nice to be out of the house.

Brain fog is mostly gone. I can concentrate at work again properly — finished a whole report last week without losing focus. Small thing but it means a lot.

---

August 6, 2024

Summer has been good. I've been walking 30 minutes most mornings since June and I think it's made a real difference. Sleep quality feels better. Waking up more refreshed than I was in winter.

Joint stiffness: now maybe 2-3 days a week rather than every day. When it does happen it passes in under 10 minutes. Huge improvement compared to January when it was daily and lasting 20+ minutes.

Headaches: down to maybe once a week, sometimes less. No cluster headaches like I was getting in February.

---

August 27, 2024

Had a stressful week at work and noticed fatigue crept back a bit. Also some joint achiness. Interesting to see how stress seems to affect things. After a calmer weekend I was back to feeling okay. Makes me think the lifestyle changes (walking, yoga, earlier bedtime) are more important than I realized.

Still on supplements: Vitamin D 4000 IU, iron 25mg, omega-3.

---

September 15, 2024

Hard to believe how different I feel compared to January. Back then I was dragging myself through every day. Now I'm walking 30 minutes in the morning, going to yoga weekly, sleeping well, and generally feeling like myself again.

Occasional joint stiffness still happens but it's mild and brief. Energy is consistent. Mood is much better — I feel like I have mental space again instead of everything feeling like an effort.

One thing I'm curious about: is the improvement from the supplements, the lifestyle changes, or both? Would be interesting to discuss with Dr. Levi at the next appointment.

---

October 10, 2024

November blood test coming up. Curious to see where the numbers land after 9 months of supplements and lifestyle changes. Hoping Vitamin D finally makes it into normal range with the higher dose.

Feeling well overall. Fatigue is no longer my main complaint. If anything I notice energy dipping a bit on days where I skip the morning walk, which is a good sign — it means the walk is actually doing something.

Morning joint stiffness: rare now, maybe once or twice a week and only lasting a few minutes.

---

October 29, 2024

Had the blood test done on November 14 (they scheduled it a couple weeks out). Writing this beforehand to capture how I'm feeling: genuinely good. Whatever combination of the Vitamin D increase, iron supplementation, the omega-3, the morning walks, and yoga — something worked. I feel like a different person compared to how I started this year.

Looking forward to seeing the numbers confirm what my body seems to already be telling me.
